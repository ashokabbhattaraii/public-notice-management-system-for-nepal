## 🎨 Design System

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Font-bold, text-lg to text-5xl
- **Body**: text-base to text-lg, muted-foreground
- **Labels**: text-xs to text-sm, uppercase variants

### Spacing
- **Padding**: 4 → 8 → 12 → 16 → 24 (consistent scale)
- **Gap**: 4 → 6 → 8 → 12 (tight to loose)
- **Margin**: Follows padding scale

### Colors
- **Primary**: Purple (oklch 0.52 0.2 280)
- **Accent**: Magenta (oklch 0.68 0.2 320)
- **Success**: Green (oklch 0.7 0.15 120)
- **Warning**: Orange (oklch 0.75 0.15 240)
- **Destructive**: Red (oklch 0.62 0.22 25)

### Interactions
- **Hover**: Opacity 80%, color shift, scale-up
- **Active**: Full opacity, primary color
- **Disabled**: Opacity 50%, muted color
- **Loading**: Skeleton animation
